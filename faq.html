FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer mobile appointments?

Yes. Mobile options are available across Greater Cincinnati and the Tri-State (OH/KY/IN) by appointment.

What ID do I need to bring?

Bring a valid government-issued photo ID. Some services may require additional documentation depending on workflow requirements.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Use the booking form on the Book page or call 513-877-6620.

Do you do DOT testing?

We provide DOT-related testing coordination as applicable. When booking, include your employer’s requirements so we can confirm the correct process.

Do you provide results?

Testing and result reporting are handled by the selected laboratory/provider according to their policies and applicable requirements for your test type.
